About Us

We craft tailored hiring and HR consulting strategies to drive your business forward.

Established in 2025, NIyantrixAI began as a collaborative effort by a team of seasoned professionals with strong backgrounds in technology, finance, and human resource management..

We specialize in delivering high-quality corporate recruitment solutions and end-to-end HR consulting services for organizations of all scales and across various industries.

At the heart of NIyantrixAI is a team of skilled and passionate professionals. With decades of combined experience, we take pride in our ability to connect companies with top-tier talent. Whether it's technical developers, ERP specialists (both functional and technical), or leadership roles up to the C-suite, our focus is on precision and alignment with client goals.

Beyond recruitment, we also provide tailored project-based services. This includes support for asset management initiatives—helping businesses enhance resource utilization and drive ROI. Our ERP implementation expertise enables us to guide organizations through smooth deployments, followed by continuous support to ensure long-term success.

We are deeply committed to quality, client satisfaction, and results. In today’s fast-changing business environment, we understand the complexities organizations face. That’s why we offer custom-fit solutions that address real-world challenges and opportunities.

At NIyantrixAI, we see ourselves as more than just a vendor — we're a long-term partner in your journey. Our industry knowledge, hands-on experience, and consultative approach empower clients to grow strategically and sustain long-term success.
